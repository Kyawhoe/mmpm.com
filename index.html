About Us

MMPM Laboratory Supply and Consultancy Services Co,Ltd
MMPM Laboratory Supply & Consultancy Service is co-founded by well-known Myanmar citizens who had been working as the senior management staff members for almost 20 years in the business firms which are doing marketing, distribution and servicing of laboratory equipment in Myanmar.

Because of the familiarity with the market, we have a good understanding of the needs and requirements of the customers with respect to the quality of laboratory equipment, the technologies and their applications. In combination with our market intelligent, knowledge and skills in usage, we believe that we will be able to deliver value-added services to our customers that are worth more than our customers deserve and that our customers will be satisfied.
